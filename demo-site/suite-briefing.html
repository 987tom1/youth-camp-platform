01 · Weekly

Who's got eyes on whom — this week?

Every week the same questions: who's leading which young people, who slipped through, who hasn't been seen in a while. Answered today in someone's head, a group chat, and a spreadsheet that's already out of date.

  • Coverage is invisible — easy for a student to belong to no one.
  • "At-risk" only surfaces once someone's already gone.
  • Every leader keeps their own list; nothing lines up.
01 · Weekly · the tool

Cloud allocation app

Allocate young people to leaders, see coverage at a glance, and let attendance do the watching for you — so nothing slips between Sundays.

  • Allocate youth ↔ leaders by grade; every student has someone.
  • "My Youth" — each leader sees their people and their gaps.
  • Attendance analytics underneath: overview, trends, at-risk, search.
  • Export to Excel for planning and follow-up.
● Live — youth-allocation-app.vercel.appRole logins: leader · grade leader · admin

02 · Each term

Step back — where are we actually tracking?

Week-to-week keeps the ship moving; every so often you need to climb the mast. A clear, honest read of the ministry — strengths, soft spots, who's drawing closer and who's drifting — to lead from.

  • The data exists, scattered across five different exports.
  • No one view of a person's journey from first visit to belonging.
  • Building that picture by hand eats a day you don't have.
02 · Each term · the tool

Visibility tool

Drop in your exports and get a periodic SWOT in minutes — one offline file, nothing installed, nothing leaves your laptop.

📊
  • Merges five CSV exports into one integration ladder — first visit → fully belonging.
  • Flags Rising and Declining connection — who to celebrate, who to chase.
  • One tap builds an executive presentation deck — your SWOT, ready to share.
  • Runs entirely in the browser; sensitive data never leaves the device.

The single biggest operation of the year — months of sign-ups, payments and blue cards, then four days on the ground where everything has to just work. Today: a stack of shared spreadsheets and a lot of crossed fingers.

03 · Each year · the tool

Camp tool

One phone-first app the whole camp runs on — it changes with the season, from the sign-up months to the four days on site.

  • One app, two modes — admin flips from pre-camp prep to live operations in one tap.
  • One record per person: registration, payments, blue cards, accommodation, check-in.
  • Accountable, audited sign-out — safeguarding built in, not bolted on.
  • Twice-daily check-in, notes, scoped notices — all in your pocket.
