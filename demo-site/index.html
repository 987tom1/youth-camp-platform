Youth Camp Platform

The Hub and the Leaders Portal, now one app. Pre-camp: registrations, payments, leader blue cards and accommodation. On the ground: daily check-in, student notes, camp sign-out/sign-in, search and zone/camp notices. An admin flips the whole app between modes — tap the badge in the header to switch.

Allocation My Youth

Youth Allocation Platform

Connects attendance to pastoral action. Every leader owns a list of students; grade and quad leaders allocate youth and set their leaders; directors see coverage and gaps across every grade and gender. Upload attendance the way you already collect it — with an Excel export.
